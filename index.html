We coordinate, supervise and regulate health insurance activities in Ondo State.

Our Vision

A government agency responsive to ensuring its residents are fit and healthy by mitigating financial barrier to affordable and qualitative health care in order to achieve universal health coverage (UHC).

Our Mission

To promote facilitation of fair financing of Health Care costs by pooling and judicious utilization of financial resources to provide financial risk protection & cost burden sharing for people against high cost of Healthcare through various prepayments programs/products prior to falling ill and providing regulatory roles to Stakeholders, HCP, HMO, MHAS etc.
